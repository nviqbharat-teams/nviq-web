Our Story

From a co-working space in Mumbai to 10,000+ vehicles

In 2020, Rahul and Priya were sitting in BKC watching a logistics company call their drivers one by one to ask where they were. It was 2020, and this was how most Indian businesses ran their fleets.

They saw the tools that existed — clunky hardware, expensive foreign software, complicated dashboards that required a full-time analyst to operate. None of it was built for the Indian fleet manager who needs answers in seconds, not reports in days.

NViQ was the answer. Simple enough that a transport supervisor can use it on a 4G phone. Powerful enough that a 500-vehicle operation can run its entire fleet from one screen.
